Nand Kishore Industry is owned and managed by Mr. Nand Kishore Soni.
This industry mainly manufactures Kernels from watermelon seeds. The Kernel is the softer, edible part of a seed contained within its shell. The production includes: getting the raw material, seperating quality-wise and then cleaning for packaging. The industry is located at Mandore Industrial Area, Jodhpur.
