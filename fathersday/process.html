Manufacturing Process

The first step is the removal of waste products from the raw material. Then it’s moved to a machine called SEPERATOR, which works by separating the seeds according to their weight and size. After that it is striked on a machine which cut the seeds into small pieces and to remove the most upper layer which is useless. Then,it is washed for hours. The water creates the steam which helps in two ways- firstly, it helps to remove the trash and secondly it helps in smooth moving through the machines. Now,the kernel is dried in the Sun.After that it is placed under a machine in order to ensure that the light things can escape from the main part. Subsequently, it’s moved to a machine called DISTONNER. This machine makes it very clear that the seeds are separated according to there weight. Then it is shifted to the machine called SORTEX. This machine separates Seeds and Watermelon Kernels.Sortex uses sensors to detect and differentiate between kernels. Its mechanism is to search for the seeds which are of specific colors. If the colours match it allows the seeds to move forward or else it rejects it and is sent to another box. At this stage the kernels are ready to be packed and gets transported.
